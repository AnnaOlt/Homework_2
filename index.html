About Me


Hi there! My name is Anna Olt. Short and sweet, unlike me who is tall and fiesty. I am currently studying to be a full-stack developer at UCLA. I also have a full-time job, which has led me to this field. I work for my family business, which is called McRoberts Technologies. We provide risk-specific solutions for security purposes. Before last year it was called "McRoberts Protective Agency," and is the oldest security company in America! Started by my great, great, great grandfather in 1876. One hundred and Fourty-Four years later we are still in the security business, but have now moved on to technology. When I was first introduced to the software I had so many questions about aesthetics and functionality. So, I decided I would like to make the software changes on my own by and help my family's company while learning valuable skills that may eventually lead to a new career.
